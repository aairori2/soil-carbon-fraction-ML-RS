Research Gap

Why This Project Matters

Current MRV protocols rely on process-based modeling, not measured SOC dynamics. There is no validated framework integrating SOC fractions, GHG flux, and RS time series for long-term carbon projection under regenerative management.

Expanded logic: Remote sensing is used as a proxy for surface conditions rather than a direct measure of SOC fractions. Sentinel-2 captures temporal changes in bare-soil reflectance and vegetation cover, Sentinel-1 provides moisture-sensitive backscatter, and NAIP adds high-resolution spatial context for treatment zones and within-field variability. These predictors are matched to in situ POM and MAOM measurements collected on the same or nearest sampling dates so the model can learn how surface signals relate to fraction dynamics over time.
